Eastman Auto & Power Limited (EAPL) is an innovator in the energy transition space, addressing both energy generation and conversion. Founded in 2000, the company has grown into a leading energy solutions provider specializing in last mile e-mobility solutions, solar solutions, and continued energy solutions.

Eastman solutions serve all segments with power backup, energy storage, and mobility products across residential, commercial, and industrial use cases.

We have 35+ years of experience in power supply and renewable energy solutions

Group Companies

One group. Five businesses.
A complete energy ecosystem.

Each company is purpose-built around a node of the energy value chain, from cells and modules to power electronics and global delivery.

Incorporated in 2021 to address customer pain points in EV charging and power backup. Its portfolio covers Home UPS/Inverters, E-Rickshaw Chargers, and Solar Inverters.
